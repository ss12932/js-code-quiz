Quiz Rules

  1. The timer starts when you start the first question of the quiz. You will be given 2 minutes to complete the quiz.
  2. For each incorrect answer you will be deducted 10 seconds.
  3. When all questions are answered, incomplete or the timer reaches 0, the game is over.
  4. You will be able to save your initials and your highscore! Enjoy!
